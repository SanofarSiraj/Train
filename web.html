About skara

Welcome to Skara Sommarland, Scandinavia's largest water park with amusement park and go-karts.

Skara sommarland is a summer park located 8 km east of skara.
It was founded in 1984 by swedish entrepreneur bert karlsson.
That way you're never far away from the fun at skara sommarland!
The water park is one of Sweden's largest water parks with adventure pools, water slides and several pool areas.
In other words, a water adventure for the whole family!
In the amusement park there is everything from calmer carousels to fast-paced attractions and roller coasters.
The park also has playgrounds and summer activities that are suitable for children of all ages.

Accomodation and camping

Let yourself be swept along by all the fun at Scandinavia's largest water park!
There are 47 attractions - everything from fun water slides and pools to fast-paced roller coasters and whirling carousels.
Extend the adventure in the park with an overnight stay at the campsite.
Choose between our cottages, apartments, place for motorhome or caravan, and tent sites.
When you book accommodation, there is always a 2-day entrance to the adventure in Sommarland
(if you book 1 night, entrance is included on days 1 and 2 and if you book 2 nights, entrance is included on days 2 and 3)!
